How To Use The Crop Health Monitoring Tool


"Welcome to Curated Crops! Our website empowers you to make informed decisions about crop selection based on your local soil conditions. By using NASA’s satellite data, you can explore, analyze, and utilize vital information about soil health, moisture levels, and nutrient composition. Follow these simple steps to optimize your farming practices and enhance your crop yield."

Step 1: Enter Your Location Once on the homepage, locate the input field where you can enter your geographic location. You can either type in your latitude and longitude coordinates or use the interactive map to select your location.

Step 2: Submit Your Location After entering your location, click the “Submit” button. This action sends your location data to the system for analysis, retrieving important soil information specific to your area

Step 3: View Your Soil Data The website will process your location and display key soil metrics such as moisture levels, soil composition, and nutrient availability. This information is crucial for understanding the health of your soil.

Step 4: Check the Crop Tier List Next, the site generates a tier list of recommended crops that are well-suited for your soil conditions. Each crop is categorized into tiers (e.g., Tier 1: Excellent, Tier 2: Good), helping you easily identify the best options.

Step 5: Read Explanations Click on each crop in the tier list to access detailed explanations. You’ll learn why certain crops are recommended based on their requirements and how they perform in your specific soil type.

Step 6: Explore Additional Resources Take advantage of the resources section to find best practices for farming based on your soil analysis. Here, you can discover tips and strategies to enhance your farming techniques.

Step 7: Stay Informed Keep checking back for updates on new features and datasets. We’re continually enhancing the website to provide you with the best possible tools for successful farming. By following these easy steps, you can leverage the power of Curated Crops to improve your farming practices and increase your crop yield!
