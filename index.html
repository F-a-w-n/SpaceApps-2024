Welcome to Curated Crops!

Our Mission:

Our website hopes to empower the Canadian, American , and Mexican agricultural communities to easily explore, analyze, and utilize NASA’s satellite and geospatial datasets to address the issues correlated with how soil quality, soil moisture, & evaporation affect crop growth

How?

Based on the location entered, our website will analyze & generate a tier list that ranks which crops produce the most harvest regarding soil quality, soil moisture, & evaporation of the farming area selected

Why?

Our curated crops will improve farming practices to optimize production & manage resources efficiently as soil quality, soil moisture, & evaporation collectively contribute to the vulnerability of agricultural communities by impacting crop health, profitability, food security, & overall sustainability
